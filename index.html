Fun Facts About Space

  • The Sun's core temperature is about 15 million degrees Celsius (27 million degrees Fahrenheit).

  • One day on Venus is longer than its year. Venus rotates very slowly in the opposite direction to most planets.

  • Jupiter's Great Red Spot is a massive storm that has been raging for over 350 years.

  • Uranus is tilted on its side, so it appears to roll along its orbital path.

  • Neptune, the farthest planet from the Sun, has the strongest winds in the solar system, reaching speeds of 1,500 mph (2,414 km/h).
